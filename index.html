Who Is InTuition?

Tuition (n) - the act or profession of teaching.
Intuition (n) - immediate apprehension or cognition.

I am a Tallahasee native, a husband, and father of three. I completed a B.S. in Physics in 2008 and an M.S. in Science Education in 2010 both from Florida State University. For the past ten years I have been a full-time science and math teacher, and I love what I do. In my spare time I enjoy doing what I can to expand my knowledge and understanding of topics and hone my craft: working on coding projects, thinking about how to integrate programming into the courses I teach, and excellent professional development opportunities. A couple of my favorites were a week long experience at Fermilab (current home to Muon g-2, MicroBooNE, MINERvA, NOvA, and others) and a six week RET program at the National High Magnetic Field Laboratory where my group analyzed superconducting materials using an electron scanning microscope.
